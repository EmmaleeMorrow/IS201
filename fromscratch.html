Why I Love Minecraft

I love to game but Minecraft is by far my favorite. I love to build and design my worlds and colloborate with my friends and family. I also love how there is freedom within the game. It can be a chill environment or a competitive environment. It's all up to imagination.

Here are some of the things I enjoy about Minecraft:

  • Creative and survival mode building
      -I love designing my world
  • Survival mode challenges
      -I love the adrenaline of mining and trying to stay alive while achieving tasks
  • Multiplayer collaborations
      -Playing with friends is the best
  • PVP games and challenges
      -Brings in a new level of competition
  • Killing the ender dragon
      -The ultimate goal of the game!!
